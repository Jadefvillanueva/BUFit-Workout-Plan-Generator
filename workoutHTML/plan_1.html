Very Light Weight-Loss Plan for Older Beginners with Injuries

  • Monday: Gentle Full-Body Mobility
    • Warm-Up (5-10 minutes): Seated or standing arm circles, slow marching in place
    • Workout (2 sets):
      • Chair Squats – 8 reps
      • Wall Push-ups – 8–10 reps
      • Seated Rows with Resistance Band – 10 reps
      • Standing Leg Raises – 10 per leg
    • Cool-Down: Gentle stretches (neck, shoulders, hamstrings)
  • Tuesday: Light Cardio and Core
    • Warm-Up: Gentle walk or pedal on stationary bike (5 minutes)
    • Workout:
      • Cardio: 15–20 min slow walk or pool walking
      • Core (2 rounds):
        • Seated Torso Twists – 10 reps per side
        • Marching in Place with Core Focus – 20 seconds
        • Seated Side Bends – 10 per side
    • Cool-Down: Deep breathing and light stretching
  • Wednesday: Recovery Movement
    • Gentle chair yoga or guided stretching video (20 minutes)
    • Optional: Easy walk around the block
  • Thursday: Upper Body Mobility & Light Cardio
    • Warm-Up: Arm swings and shoulder rolls
    • Workout:
      • Strength (2 sets):
        • Wall Push-ups – 10 reps
        • Seated Overhead Press (light weights or bands) – 8 reps
        • Elbow Extensions (with band) – 10 reps
      • Cardio: 15 min of alternating slow and brisk walking (1 min each)
    • Cool-Down: Shoulder and arm stretches
  • Friday: Lower Body Strength
    • Warm-Up: Gentle leg swings and ankle circles
    • Workout (2 sets):
      • Sit-to-Stand from Chair – 8 reps
      • Standing Calf Raises – 12 reps
      • Glute Squeeze (seated or lying) – 10 reps
      • Heel Slides (on floor or bed) – 10 reps per leg
    • Cool-Down: Stretch calves, thighs, and hips
  • Saturday: Enjoyable Movement
    • Choose a fun light activity: gardening, slow dance, scenic walk, or aquatic therapy (20–30 minutes)
  • Sunday: Rest or Very Gentle Recovery
    • Deep breathing, light stretching, or meditation (15 minutes)
    • Optional: Gentle walk for circulation
    • Tips:
      • Morning routines help build consistency
      • Use chairs, walls, or rails for balance and support
      • Focus on comfort over intensity; avoid pain or strain
      • Stay hydrated and eat nourishing, easy-to-digest meals
      • Celebrate small wins and listen to your body
