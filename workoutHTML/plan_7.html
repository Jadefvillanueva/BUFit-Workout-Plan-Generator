Advanced Muscle-Gain Evening Plan

  • Monday: Upper Body Hypertrophy
    • Warm-Up: Arm circles, shoulder mobility (5 min), treadmill brisk walk or light jog (5 min)
    • Workout (4 sets each):
      • Barbell Bench Press - 8-10 reps
      • Incline Dumbbell Press - 10 reps
      • Bent-over Rows - 10 reps
      • Overhead Shoulder Press - 10 reps
      • EZ Bar Curls - 12 reps
      • Tricep Pushdowns - 12 reps
    • Cool-Down: Chest and shoulder static stretching (5 min)
  • Tuesday: Lower Body Power
    • Warm-Up: Leg swings, dynamic squats (5 min), light cycling (5 min)
    • Workout (4 sets each):
      • Back Squats - 6-8 reps
      • Romanian Deadlifts - 8 reps
      • Leg Press - 12 reps
      • Walking Lunges - 12 reps per leg
      • Standing Calf Raises - 15 reps
    • Cool-Down: Hamstring, quad, and calf stretches (5 min)
  • Wednesday: Rest or Active Recovery
    • Foam rolling or light stretching (20 min)
    • Optional: Evening walk or yoga
  • Thursday: Push (Chest/Shoulders/Triceps)
    • Warm-Up: Dynamic push-ups, arm swings (5 min)
    • Workout (4 sets each):
      • Dumbbell Chest Press - 10 reps
      • Arnold Press - 10 reps
      • Cable Flys - 12 reps
      • Lateral Raises - 15 reps
      • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extensions - 12 reps
    • Cool-Down: Triceps and shoulder stretches (5 min)
  • Friday: Pull (Back/Biceps)
    • Warm-Up: Arm circles, light rows (5 min)
    • Workout (4 sets each):
      • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown - 10 reps
      • Seated Rows - 12 reps
      • Face Pulls - 15 reps
      • Barbell Curls - 12 reps
      • Hammer Curls - 12 reps
    • Cool-Down: Biceps and upper back stretches (5 min)
  • Saturday: Full-Body Conditioning
    • Warm-Up: Dynamic full-body stretches (5 min), jump rope or light jog (5 min)
    • Workout (Circuit - 3 rounds):
      • Burpees - 12 reps
      • Kettlebell Swings - 15 reps
      • Mountain Climbers - 30 sec
      • Push-ups - 12 reps
      • Jump Squats - 15 reps
    • Cool-Down: Stretch all major muscle groups (5-10 min)
  • Sunday: Rest and Recovery Tips
    • Focus on hydration, high-protein meals
    • Optional: Light walk or meditation
    • Log your progress and prepare for the week ahead
