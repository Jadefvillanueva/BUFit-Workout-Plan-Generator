Intermediate Yoga/Pilates Flexibility Plan

  • Monday: Core-Focused Pilates
    • Warm-Up (5-10 minutes): Gentle seated twists, cat-cow stretches, diaphragmatic breathing
    • Workout (3 rounds):
      • Hundred – 30 seconds
      • Leg Circles – 10 reps each leg
      • Single Leg Stretch – 12 reps
      • Double Leg Stretch – 10 reps
      • Plank to Downward Dog – 8 reps
    • Cool-Down: Supine spinal twist, Child’s Pose
  • Tuesday: Flow Yoga (Vinyasa Style)
    • Warm-Up: Sun Salutation A – 3 rounds
    • Workout (Flow Sequence):
      • Warrior I → Warrior II → Reverse Warrior
      • Chair Pose → Forward Fold → Plank
      • Cobra → Downward Dog → Low Lunge (both sides)
      • Repeat Flow 3x
    • Cool-Down: Pigeon Pose (each side), Seated Forward Fold
  • Wednesday: Rest or Gentle Stretching
    • 10–15 minutes of breath-focused stretching
    • Optional: Try Yin Yoga or Somatic Movement
  • Thursday: Pilates for Flexibility and Control
    • Warm-Up: Shoulder rolls, spine articulation
    • Workout (3 sets):
      • Roll-Up – 10 reps
      • Saw – 10 reps
      • Swimming – 30 seconds
      • Side Kicks – 12 each side
      • Pelvic Curl – 12 reps
    • Cool-Down: Hamstring stretches with band, Figure Four Stretch
  • Friday: Yoga Balance and Stability
    • Warm-Up: Hip openers, standing stretches
    • Workout (3 rounds):
      • Tree Pose – 30 sec each side
      • Half Moon – 20 sec each side
      • Eagle Pose – 20 sec each side
      • Chair to Airplane Pose – 8 transitions
    • Cool-Down: Happy Baby Pose, Supine Twist, Savasana
  • Saturday: Explore a New Style
    • Try a different yoga or Pilates class (online or in-person)
    • Suggestions: Kundalini, Barre, Power Yoga, Tai Chi-Pilates fusion
  • Sunday: Rest and Reflection
    • Gentle stretching or restorative poses (15 minutes)
    • Journal reflections on the week’s movement experiences
