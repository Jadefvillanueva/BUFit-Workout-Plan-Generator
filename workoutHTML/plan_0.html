Plan 0 – 3-Day Full-Body Foundation

  • Monday – Full-Body Strength
    • Warm-Up (5–10 min): Dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings); Light cardio (jumping jacks, jog in place).
    • Workout (3 sets each):
      • Squats (body-weight or goblet) – 12 reps [Tutorial]
      • Push-ups (knee or full) – 8–12 reps [Tutorial]
      • Bent-over rows (resistance band or dumbbells) – 10–12 reps [Tutorial]
      • Plank – hold 30–45 s [Tutorial]
    • Cool-Down (5 min): Static stretches (hamstrings, quads, shoulders).
  • Tuesday – Active Recovery
    • Yoga flow or full-body stretch (20–30 min) [Tutorial]
    • Easy walk or light cycling (15–20 min).
  • Wednesday – Pull + Mobility
    • Warm-Up (5 min): Cat–cow + child's pose (2×30 s each).
    • Workout (3 sets each):
      • Resistance-band or dumbbell rows – 12 reps/arm [Tutorial]
      • Reverse flys (bands or light dumbbells) – 12–15 reps [Tutorial]
      • Good mornings (body-weight or light DB) – 12 reps [Tutorial]
    • Cool-Down (5 min): Shoulder and upper-back stretches.
  • Thursday – Rest or Optional Cardio
    • Brisk walk or slow jog (20–30 min) [Inspiration]
    • Foam rolling or gentle stretching (10–15 min) [Tutorial]
  • Friday – Legs + Cardio
    • Warm-Up (5 min): Leg swings + hip circles.
    • Workout:
      • Body-weight lunges – 3×10 reps/leg [Tutorial]
      • Glute bridges – 3×12 reps [Tutorial]
      • Calf raises – 3×15 reps [Tutorial]
      • Cardio finisher: 10 min HIIT (30 s sprint/30 s walk) [Tutorial]
    • Cool-Down (5 min): Quad and calf stretches.
  • Saturday – Core & Mobility
    • Russian twists – 3×20 reps [Tutorial]
    • Bicycle crunches – 3×20 reps [Tutorial]
    • Side plank – 3×20–30 s/side [Tutorial]
    • Thoracic rotations + pigeon pose (2×30 s each) [Tutorial]
  • Sunday – Rest or Gentle Recovery
    • Light yoga, foam rolling, or walking (15–20 min) [Routine]
