Beginner Muscle-Gain Plan Using Dumbbells

  • Monday: Full-Body Dumbbell Strength
    • Warm-Up (5–10 minutes): Arm circles, hip circles, light jogging or step marching
    • Workout (3 sets each):
      • Dumbbell Goblet Squats – 10 reps
      • Dumbbell Chest Press (floor or bench) – 10 reps
      • One-Arm Dumbbell Row – 10 reps per side
      • Standing Dumbbell Curl – 10 reps
    • Cool-Down: Stretch quads, shoulders, and back
  • Tuesday: Cardio & Core
    • Warm-Up (5 minutes): Light jog or dynamic stretches
    • Workout:
      • Cardio: 20–30 min walk, bike, or low-impact step
      • Core (3 rounds):
        • Dumbbell Russian Twists – 10 per side
        • Dead Bug (bodyweight) – 10 reps
        • Forearm Plank – 20–30 seconds
    • Cool-Down: Breathing exercises and light stretching
  • Wednesday: Active Recovery
    • 20–30 minutes of yoga or stretching
    • Optional: Leisure walk or easy recreational activity
  • Thursday: Upper Body Dumbbell Strength
    • Warm-Up: Arm swings, shoulder rolls, light cardio (5 mins)
    • Workout (3 sets each):
      • Dumbbell Overhead Press – 10 reps
      • Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 10 reps
      • Dumbbell Tricep Kickbacks – 10 reps per arm
      • Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 10 reps
    • Cool-Down: Stretch chest, arms, and shoulders
  • Friday: Lower Body Dumbbell Strength
    • Warm-Up: Same as Monday
    • Workout (3 sets each):
      • Dumbbell Lunges – 10 reps per leg
      • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts – 10 reps
      • Dumbbell Calf Raises – 15 reps
      • Glute Bridges (bodyweight or with dumbbell) – 12 reps
    • Cool-Down: Stretch calves, hamstrings, and glutes
  • Saturday: Optional Fun Movement or Light Cardio
    • Choose an activity you enjoy: dance, walk, or sports (30–45 minutes)
  • Sunday: Rest or Recovery
    • Light stretching, foam rolling, or deep breathing (15–20 minutes)
    • Tips:
      • Focus on learning proper form before increasing weight
      • Eat protein-rich meals around workouts (e.g., eggs, chicken, beans)
      • Stay hydrated and sleep well for better recovery
      • Track your progress to stay motivated
      • Don't skip rest days—they're key for muscle growth
